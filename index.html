Welcome to Village Winery

Where it all begins

Tradition of grape winemaking in our family began in the early 80's. From our 50 acres of vineyard we made our first bottle of wine ranging from Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Sauvignon Blanc to Chardonnay and manyothers.
Since then, our passion for making quality wines grew bigger and better. In 1991 we founded Village Winery and welcomed countless numbers of happy wine lovers. The wines we produced have been served in almost every part of the world. If you are in search of a glass of a fine wine, whether you prefer red,white or rose, make sure to check what we can offer.
We are looking forward to welcoming you.

Wine-tasting Tours

Enjoy our unique and unforgettable wine-tasting tour experience in scenic villages and vineyards. Taste one of the best selections of wines in our over 100 years old cellars, made with passion,traditionally by our family. Learn about the grape wine making process and what makes our wines specific. If you wish to arange wine-tasting experience tour please let us know.
